PROJECT 2

RECTIFIER LAB DIODES & RECTIFIED SIGNALS

Introduction

This experiment helps students get familiar with the combinations of resistors, capacitors, and diodes. Also, students can learn how to use a function generator and oscilloscope. The student will use them to rectify signals and convert AC signals to DC signals. Especially, students will keep practicing using the PSPICE software or circuitlab.com to simulate the circuit.

Result

Conclusion

Related course work: Assembly Language Programming, Algorithms and Data Structures Design, Engineering Statistics, Software Engineering I, Electronics for Computing Systems, Digital Design I, Advanced Algorithm Design.

Thanks to this experience, students know about the characteristic of the diode. In addition, students have learned about how to assembly diode into the circuit to convert the AC to DC and rectify the signal. Finally, students can practice with the PSpice software or circuilab.com to construct and simulate to get the result, especially new things about output signal cross capacitor.
